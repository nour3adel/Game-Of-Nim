Inctructions and help

Players take turns taking chips, and the last person to take a chip wins. Each turn a player is allowed to take one to three of the chips if piles equals 2before ending their turn. Once a player has chosen their desired chips they can end their turn to give the other player control. This continues between both players until one has to take the last chip. Whoever takes the final chip is declared the winner.
